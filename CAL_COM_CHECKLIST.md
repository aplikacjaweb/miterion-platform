# Cal.com Panel Configuration Checklist

## Steps to Complete in Your Cal.com Panel

### 1. Privacy Policy Consent (RODO/GDPR)
- **Location:** Booking Questions → Add new checkbox
- **Settings:**
  - Required: ✅ (checked)
  - Question text: "I agree to the Privacy Policy"
  - Link to your privacy policy

### 2. Minimum Notice Time
- **Location:** Event Type Settings → Scheduling
- **Setting:** Minimum notice time = 2 hours
- **Purpose:** Prevents last-minute bookings

### 3. Buffer Time
- **Location:** Event Type Settings → Scheduling
- **Setting:** Buffer time after meeting = 15 minutes
- **Purpose:** Provides breathing room between meetings

### 4. Availability
- **Location:** Availability Settings
- **Action:** Set your working hours and time zones
- **Recommendation:** Match your business hours

### 5. Confirmation & Reminder Emails
- **Location:** Email Notifications
- **Settings:**
  - Booking confirmation: ✅ (enabled)
  - Reminder emails: ✅ (enabled, 24h before)
  - Cancellation emails: ✅ (enabled)

### 6. Branding (Optional)
- **Location:** Profile Settings → Branding
- **Settings:**
  - Primary color: #000000 (black)
  - Logo: Upload your company logo
  - Company name: Your business name

## Verification
After completing these steps:
1. Test the booking flow from your website
2. Verify that the privacy policy checkbox appears
3. Confirm that minimum notice and buffer times are respected
4. Check that confirmation emails are sent correctly

The floating widget should now be fully functional with proper privacy compliance!
